This classic lasanga recipe is a family favorite.
You'll have the entire family happy with this recipe!
This is a classic homemade lasagna recipe made with layers of gooey melted mozzarella cheese,
a parmesan ricotta cheese, and a robust tomato meat sauce.
It is perfect for weeknight dinners, potlucks, or to take to friends who just had a baby.
This lasagna recipe can be frozen so it is perfect to made ahead of time. It also makes flavorful leftovers.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Pepper
  • 32-40oz Jar Marinara Sauce

Parmesan Rioctta Cheese Filling:

  1. 2 Cups Whole Milk Ricotta Cheese
  2. 1 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  3. 1 Large Egg
  4. 1 Teaspoon Dried Basil
  5. 3/4 Teaspoon Salt
  6. 1/2 Teaspoon Pepper
  7. 12 lasagna Noodles
  8. 4 Cups Mozzarella Cheese
